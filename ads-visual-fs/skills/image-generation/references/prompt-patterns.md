# Image Generation Prompt Patterns

## Pattern: Product-Forward Ad

```
Professional marketing ad for [PRODUCT]. Clean, modern layout with [BRAND COLOR] background.
Large headline text "[H1]" in Poppins SemiBold at top.
Support line "[SUPPORT]" in Inter Regular below.
[CTA] button in [ACCENT COLOR] at bottom-center.
[PRODUCT VISUAL DESCRIPTION] as the central element.
Trust badge showing "[TRUST SIGNAL]" near the CTA.
Small regulatory text "[DISCLAIMER]" at the bottom.
Professional, trustworthy, empowering mood. High contrast for readability.
Do not include gambling, casino, rockets, memes, or illegible text.
```

## Pattern: Lifestyle Ad

```
Editorial-style marketing image for [PRODUCT]. Shows [SUBJECT DESCRIPTION] in [SETTING].
Natural lighting, warm tones with brand accents in [BRAND COLORS].
Overlay text: "[H1]" in Poppins SemiBold, positioned [POSITION].
Subtle CTA "[CTA]" in [ACCENT COLOR] button.
Authentic, professional, Southeast Asian business context.
[TRUST SIGNALS] integrated naturally.
Do not include generic stock aesthetics, gambling, or memes.
```

## Pattern: Data-Driven Ad

```
Clean infographic-style marketing ad for [PRODUCT]. [BRAND COLOR] background.
Large stat or number as hero element: "[KEY STAT]".
Headline "[H1]" in Poppins SemiBold.
Supporting data points: [DATA POINTS].
CTA "[CTA]" in prominent [ACCENT COLOR] button.
Professional, data-forward, trustworthy. Minimal decoration.
Small disclaimer: "[DISCLAIMER]".
Do not include cluttered layouts, illegible text, or gambling imagery.
```

## Complete Example: Filled Product-Forward Prompt

```
Professional marketing ad for Funding Societies SME Business Term Loan. Clean, modern layout
with purple #5203EA background gradient transitioning to blue #2C50FF at the bottom.
Large headline text "Grow Your Business Without Limits" in Poppins SemiBold, white, centered
in the top third. Support line "Over 10,000 SMEs funded across Southeast Asia" in Inter Regular,
light gray #F1F1F2, below the headline. Yellow #FFDE0F "Apply Now" CTA button at bottom-center
with rounded corners. Professional photo of a confident SME owner in a modern office as the
central element, slightly right of center. Teal #27E4CD trust badge showing "Licensed by MAS
(CMS101541)" near the CTA. Small regulatory text "Funding Societies Pte Ltd holds a Capital
Markets Services Licence (CMS101541)" in Inter Regular, small, at the very bottom.
Professional, trustworthy, empowering mood. High contrast for readability. Clean composition
with adequate whitespace.
Brand colors: Light Gray #F1F1F2, Yellow #FFDE0F, Purple #5203EA, Teal #27E4CD, Blue #2C50FF.
Typography: Poppins SemiBold for headings, Inter Regular for body.
Tone: professional, trustworthy, empowering.
Do not include: gambling, casino, rockets, memes, aggressive lending, predatory language,
illegible text, distorted faces, or generic stock photo aesthetics.
```

## FS Brand Color Usage

| Color | Hex | Best Used For |
|-------|-----|--------------|
| Light Gray | #F1F1F2 | Backgrounds, neutral areas |
| Yellow | #FFDE0F | CTA buttons, highlights, urgency |
| Purple | #5203EA | Primary brand, headings, hero backgrounds |
| Teal | #27E4CD | Secondary accents, data visualizations |
| Blue | #2C50FF | Links, interactive elements, trust |
