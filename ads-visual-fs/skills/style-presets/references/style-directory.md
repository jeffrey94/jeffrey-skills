# Style Directory

4 predefined visual styles for FS ad creatives. Each style defines required elements, composition, layout, and mood. Colors are NOT fixed — use the FS brand palette flexibly based on campaign context.

Reference images are bundled alongside this file. **Read the reference image for the selected style** before generating concept prompts.

---

## Style 1 — Warm Showcase

**Reference image:** `references/style-1-warm-showcase.jpg`

### Required Elements
- Photo-realistic person in their real work environment (shop, stall, workshop)
- 3D rendered props relevant to their trade scattered around them (tools, products, equipment, oversized coins)
- Large organic blob shape as a framing device behind the subject
- Solid color background (from FS brand palette)

### Composition
- Person is the hero — standing confidently, facing camera, candid but composed
- 3D props create depth and narrative context around the person
- Organic blob shape sits behind or partially overlaps the person, creating visual separation from background
- Mix of photographic realism (person) and 3D illustration (props, blob shape)

### Photography Direction
- Warm, natural lighting with slight golden cast
- Candid pose — person mid-action or genuinely smiling at camera
- Real workplace context visible (shop counter, market stall, workshop bench)
- Photo-real person composited into a styled scene with 3D elements

### Layout (from reference — prioritize this)
- Headline: top area, large bold text
- Subject + props: center, occupying ~60% of frame
- Organic blob shape: behind subject, spanning mid-frame
- Support line: below headline or above CTA
- CTA button: bottom-center, pill shape
- Disclaimer: very bottom, small text

### Mood
Warm, friendly, approachable. "This could be you" energy — relatable, not aspirational. Celebrates micro-SME owners in their element.

### Prompt Injection Fragment
```
Visual style: "Warm Showcase" — Photo-realistic person standing confidently in their real work environment, surrounded by 3D rendered props relevant to their trade (tools, products, equipment). A large organic blob shape in an FS brand color serves as a framing device behind the subject. Solid color background from FS brand palette. Warm natural lighting with golden cast. Mix of photographic realism (person) and 3D illustration (props, decorative elements). Layout: headline at top, person with props center, CTA button bottom-center. Friendly, relatable micro-SME energy.
```

## Style 2 — Bold Professional

**Reference image:** `references/style-2-bold-professional.png`

### Required Elements
- Photo-realistic person in their workplace (workshop, office, store)
- Teal rounded-rectangle as a photo mask/frame around the person and their environment
- Trust badge pill element (e.g., "100,000+ SMEs funded" with checkmark icon)
- Gradient background (using FS brand colors)

### Composition
- Person as hero, shown in context of their workplace through the teal photo mask
- The rounded-rectangle photo mask creates a window into the workplace scene
- Background environment visible through/around the mask adds depth
- Trust badge pill floats as an overlay element, reinforcing credibility
- Clean separation between text areas (on gradient) and photo area (in mask)

### Photography Direction
- Professional, well-lit workplace photography
- Person facing camera with confident posture
- Real work environment visible (equipment, colleagues in background)
- High production quality — polished but not sterile

### Layout (from reference — prioritize this)
- Headline: top-left, large bold text with one word in accent color
- Trust badge: top-center, pill shape with checkmark
- Logo: top-right
- Photo mask: center, teal rounded-rectangle containing person + workplace
- Support line: below photo mask area
- CTA button: bottom-center, yellow pill
- Disclaimer: very bottom, small text

### Mood
Corporate, polished, established. Signals trust, scale, and professionalism. For businesses that want to be taken seriously.

### Prompt Injection Fragment
```
Visual style: "Bold Professional" — Photo-realistic person in their workplace, framed within a teal rounded-rectangle photo mask. The mask creates a window into the workplace scene with colleagues and equipment visible. Gradient background using FS brand colors. A trust badge pill element (e.g., "100,000+ SMEs funded" with checkmark) floats as an overlay. Layout: bold headline top-left with one accent-color word, trust badge pill top-center, logo top-right, photo mask center, support line below, CTA bottom-center. Polished, corporate, high-credibility feel.
```

## Style 3 — Tech Forward

**Reference image:** `references/style-3-tech-forward.png`

### Required Elements
- Photo-realistic person in foreground
- Phone or device mockup showing a "future state" or growth scene (e.g., expanded business, approval screen)
- Person visually breaks out of or emerges from the device frame into the foreground
- Gradient background (using FS brand colors)

### Composition
- Conceptual composition: person exists in two planes — the digital (inside device) and the physical (foreground)
- Phone/device mockup is prominent, showing what the business could become or the app experience
- Person breaks the fourth wall of the device, reaching toward the viewer
- Real workplace environment visible in background, blending with the device content
- Creates a narrative of digital transformation — "your phone unlocks your business potential"

### Photography Direction
- Cinematic lighting with depth — foreground person sharp, background softer
- Person in work attire, looking directly at camera or at their phone
- Device screen shows a compelling scene (growing business, loan approval, dashboard)
- Blend of photographic foreground and rendered device content

### Layout (from reference — prioritize this)
- Logo: top-right
- Headline: top-left, large bold text
- Support copy: below headline, smaller text
- Person + device mockup: center, occupying ~50-60% of frame
- CTA button: bottom-center, yellow pill
- Disclaimer: very bottom, small text

### Mood
Digital transformation, growth, innovation. "Technology empowers your business." Forward-looking, modern, fintech-native.

### Prompt Injection Fragment
```
Visual style: "Tech Forward" — Photo-realistic person in foreground with a phone/device mockup showing a future-state scene (expanded business, loan approval screen). The person visually breaks out of or emerges from the device frame. Gradient background using FS brand colors. Real workplace environment blends with device content. Layout: logo top-right, bold headline top-left, support copy below, person with device mockup center, CTA bottom-center. Digital transformation energy — technology empowers business growth.
```

## Style 4 — Aspirational Shadow

**Reference image:** `references/style-4-aspirational-shadow.png`

### Required Elements
- 3D rendered subject representing the SME's current state (food cart, market stall, small shop, sewing machine)
- Shadow cast by the subject depicting the "dream" version (bigger store, restaurant, warehouse, factory, fleet)
- Clean light/neutral background (white, light gray from FS palette)
- Subtle pastel gradient accents

### Composition
- The 3D rendered subject is the focal point — detailed, charming, realistic
- The shadow is the storytelling device — it's larger, showing the aspirational future
- Shadow should be clearly recognizable as a bigger/upgraded version of the subject
- Person may be present (inside the cart/stall) but is secondary to the metaphor
- Minimal, editorial composition — lots of white space, clean lines
- The visual metaphor carries the entire message without needing explanation

### Photography Direction
- 3D rendered/illustrated style for the subject (not photographic)
- Studio-like lighting — clean, even, soft shadows (except the metaphor shadow)
- The dream-shadow has a distinct visual treatment (silhouette, slightly transparent, different color from FS palette)
- High production quality — feels like a premium editorial illustration

### Layout (from reference — prioritize this)
- Headline: top area, large text in FS brand color
- Support line: below headline
- 3D subject: center-left, with dream-shadow extending behind/right
- Logo + key stat (if applicable): right of subject
- CTA button: bottom-center, yellow pill
- Disclaimer: very bottom, small text

### Mood
Minimal, editorial, aspirational. "Dream bigger — we'll fund the gap." The shadow tells the story of where you're going. Emotional, not transactional.

### Prompt Injection Fragment
```
Visual style: "Aspirational Shadow" — 3D rendered subject (food cart, stall, small shop) on a clean white/light gray background. The subject casts an enlarged shadow depicting the dream version (bigger store, warehouse, restaurant). The shadow is the storytelling device — clearly recognizable as an upgraded version. Subtle pastel gradient accents from FS brand palette. Studio-like lighting. Layout: headline top, support line below, 3D subject center-left with dream-shadow extending behind, logo right of subject, CTA bottom-center. Minimal editorial feel — aspirational, emotional, "dream bigger."
```

## Custom

When the user selects "Custom", prompt for a free-text style description:

> "Describe the visual style you want — include subject treatment (photo-realistic, 3D, illustrated), framing devices (shapes, masks, mockups), composition approach (minimal, dense, editorial), and mood."

Use the user's description directly as the style direction in concept prompts. Wrap it in the same prompt injection structure as predefined styles.
