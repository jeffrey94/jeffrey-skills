# Platform Specifications

## Social Media

### Instagram

| Format | Dimensions | Aspect Ratio | Notes |
|--------|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Feed Post | 1080×1080 | 1:1 | Square, most versatile |
| Story / Reel | 1080×1920 | 9:16 | Avoid top 14% and bottom 20% |

### Facebook

| Format | Dimensions | Aspect Ratio | Notes |
|--------|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Feed Post | 1200×900 | 4:3 | Landscape preferred |
| Story | 1080×1920 | 9:16 | Same safe zones as IG Story |

### LinkedIn

| Format | Dimensions | Aspect Ratio | Notes |
|--------|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Feed Post | 1200×627 | 4:3 | Professional tone |
| Story | 1080×1920 | 9:16 | B2B context |

### TikTok

| Format | Dimensions | Aspect Ratio | Notes |
|--------|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Video Cover | 1080×1920 | 9:16 | Bold, high-contrast |

## Display & Video

### Google Display Network

| Format | Dimensions | Aspect Ratio | Notes |
|--------|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Leaderboard | 728×90 | 16:9 | Minimal copy |
| Medium Rectangle | 300×250 | 4:3 | Most common |
| Wide Skyscraper | 160×600 | 9:16 | Vertical sidebar |

### YouTube

| Format | Dimensions | Aspect Ratio | Notes |
|--------|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Thumbnail | 1280×720 | 16:9 | Bold text, faces |

## Safe Zones (9:16 Story Format)

```
┌──────────────┐
│   TOP 14%    │ ← Username, camera UI
│              │
│  SAFE ZONE   │ ← Main content (66%)
│              │
│ BOTTOM 20%   │ ← CTA button, swipe up
└──────────────┘
```
