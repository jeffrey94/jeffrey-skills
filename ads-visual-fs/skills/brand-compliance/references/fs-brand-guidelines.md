# Funding Societies Brand Guidelines (FSMY, May 2025)

## Company Overview

**Funding Societies | Modalku** is Southeast Asia's largest SME digital financing platform. Licensed and registered in Singapore (MAS), Malaysia (SC), Indonesia (OJK), Thailand (SEC), and Vietnam (SBV).

### Strapline

- **"Stronger SMEs, Stronger Societies"**
- 2-line layout: no punctuation between lines
- Horizontal layout: comma separator, no full stop
  - ✓ "Stronger SMEs, Stronger Societies"
  - ✗ "Stronger SMEs, Stronger Societies."

### Logo

**Primary logotype**: 2-line format for marketing collaterals
**Alternative logotype**: horizontal format for e-greetings and wider formats

**Approved versions**:
- Full color on white background
- Full color on dark background
- Mono white (on colored backgrounds)
- Mono dark (on light backgrounds)

**On image backgrounds**: colored logo encouraged; use semi-transparent overlay if background is busy

**Clear space**: minimum 1× the logo mark width on ALL sides

**Logo Do's**:
- Maintain adequate clear space
- Use approved color versions only
- Ensure legibility at all sizes
- Use high-resolution files
- Place on approved background types

**Logo Don'ts**:
- Do not alter proportions or stretch
- Do not add shadows, gradients, or bevels
- Do not use unapproved colors
- Do not place on busy backgrounds without overlay
- Do not add decorative elements to the logo
- Do not rotate or skew

### Tone of Voice

- **Professional** — Licensed financial institution, not a startup
- **Trustworthy** — Data-backed claims, regulatory compliance front and center
- **Empowering** — SMEs are the heroes; FS is the enabler
- **Accessible** — Complex finance made simple, no jargon
- **Regional** — Respect for Southeast Asian business culture and diversity

### Mandatory Don'ts

- Never use gambling, casino, or get-rich-quick imagery
- Never promise guaranteed returns or risk-free investments
- Never use meme formats or rocket emojis for financial content
- Never alter the logo colors or proportions
- Never use generic stock photos unrelated to SMEs
- Never make claims without regulatory disclaimers
- Never use aggressive or predatory lending language
